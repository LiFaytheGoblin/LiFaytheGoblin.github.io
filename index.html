General Information about Linda Fernsel

Linda is studying computer science and media. She wrote her Bachelor Thesis about Gender Equality in Computer Science Publications. A year later she followed up with her study project Algorithmic Fairness from Theory to Practical Application. A Fairness Audit of NamSor’s Gender and Ethnicity Classification Algorithms.. She has recently joined the "Computer Science and Society" ("Informatik und Gesellschaft") group, where she wrote her master thesis on Data Synthesis for Fairness Audits of Learning Analytics Systems. When Linda is not doing research she is running CoderDojo Schöneweide and teaching students about programming.
