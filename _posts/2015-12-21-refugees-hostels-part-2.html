And now for how learning German works for refugees at my refugees' hostel.

First of all: There is a huge lack of teachers. Most lessons are done by people like you and me, without the knowledge of how to teacher German as a foreign language. This is pretty bad, but better than nothing. In my refugees' hostel, there are no wifi and no computers and only few have a smartphone. So people can't really learn on their own. There's not enough books either. So after lessons, people go back to their everyday life and speak in their native language to each other. Plus German is known as a hard language and many people need to learn a whole new alphabet or have never been to a school (and thus can't read or write). Conclusion: Learning German takes long. Exception: Children. Seriousely, sending kids to school in Germany with German kids is the best thing you can do for them to learn the language quickly and to get integrated.
